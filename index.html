LAPSTICK

The Ultimate Travel Guitar

FEATURES

Lapstick travel guitar is a fully-featured short scale electric guitar that is compact enough to fit into the pocket of an overcoat.

Made with care in Germany by industry veteran Phil Neal.

Lapstick has a scale length 3/4 of a normal electric guitar and can be tuned a minor third (G) to a fifth (B) higher than normal. Smaller fret size and neck width make it a perfect practice guitar.

Lapstick is equipped with a battery-powered preamp which allows the use of headphones for situations that require silent, private practice time.
